RedFive: (check the videos and see for yourself!)
"Redfive is my continuing education in automotive engineering. I took as many courses in the field in college, but wasn't able to get a job in that field when I was done. This is my way of getting experience. That being said, I like to do things my own way. That is why I have a 5 series not a 3 series. Its totally different, one of a kind. When people talk about my car, you know what car they are mentioning, because there are no imitators. Personally, I think it is one of the best all purpose cars ever built. Just not acknowledged as such. It works great on the track, just a tad bit underpowered. If you study the design, it has many of the same design cues as a classic muscle car, which is why it looks intimidating. Yet it is refined automobile. This project is far from easy and because very little exists for the E34s, everything is a test to see if it works. Fortunately I love challenges and I have grabbed this bull by both horns. I would like to win in order to give recognition to all the people who have helped me and to show what I can do and what an E34 can do." - Justin...

What Justin said about entering the One lap of America 2002: "That has been my plan. There are a couple obstacles to overcome first.
1. I haven't been able to sign up a hot-shoe driver yet. My driver last year, can only do it if I pay him. The other guy I've been talking to can't commit until his schedule for May is resolved.
2. My car is in several hundred pieces right now as I try to get this supercharger system to fit. The next step is to get the car to run properly. Progress has been slow and the cost keeps going up. I just purchased a custom radiator, $400 worth of injectors, and my custom intercooler doesn't fit the way I would like. Parts seem to take forever in arriving.
3. I want to see the car run before I spend two grand on a nonrefundable entry fee. New Mexico is a long tow to Connecticut, should something happen.
4. My friend who promised to find me thousands of dollars in sponsorship money, hasn't done a thing.
Fortunately several sponsors from last year already re-signed for this year: Bavarian Autosport, Bavarian Engine Exchange and Southern Auto Auction."
