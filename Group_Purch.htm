Racing Dynamics sway bars: Electrodyne - RDSport and Racing Dynamics...
In 1997, Racing Dynamics SpA terminated its association with its US importer Electrodyne, Inc. and its affiliate Racing Dynamics Inc. The dispute arose over parts manufactured by Electrodyne without permission from Racing Dynamics Spa As a result, the contract was terminated, but Electrodyne refused to return the Racing Dynamics trademark registration to Racing Dynamics Spa

As a result, in 1997 Racing Dynamics Spa appointed its fully owned subsidiary, RADIUS Advanced Automotive Technologies, Inc., as the US importer for Racing Dynamics genuine products. The agreement called for RADIUS to set up a complete distribution network to make the genuine Racing Dynamics Spa products available to its US based customers.

The name chosen for the new venture, RDSport, is a tribute to the original Racing Dynamics Sports team and, more important, a loud and clear message to our loyal US customers that the efforts fielded by RADIUS are the efforts of Racing Dynamics Spa To strengthen this relationship, Federico L. Pavoncelli, founder and chairman of Racing Dynamics (a/k/a/ Mr. Racing Dynamics), relocated to California in order to closely monitor all aspects of RDSports' operations.

All Racing Dynamics customers that have enjoyed and appreciated the design and quality our products should know the engineering and development capabilities of Racing Dynamics Spa are now available through RDSport.
